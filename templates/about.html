About RiskHealthBoard

Welcome to RishkHealthBoard!, The Health Prediction system which is an end user support and online risk assessment system for medical institutions to generate risk assessments for their patients. Here we propose a system that allows users to get instant guidance on their health issues through an intelligent health care system online. The system is fed with various symptoms and the disease/illness associated with those systems. The system allows user to share their symptoms and issues. It then processes user's symptoms to check for various illnesses that could be associated with it.
